    Privacy Policy

    Last updated: April 26, 2026

    ReconKit Pro is a static, browser-only security research toolkit. The tools run locally in your browser. The app does not operate a backend server and does not intentionally collect, store, sell, or transmit the content you paste into the tools.

    Inputs such as domains, dorks, JWTs, cookies, headers, iframe HTML, secrets, URLs, and policy settings are processed client-side. Theme preferences may be saved in your browser's localStorage so the selected theme persists after reload.

    The app may use third-party assets such as Google Fonts. Your browser may request those assets directly from their providers, and those providers may receive standard web request metadata such as IP address, user agent, and referrer.

    Buttons that open Google, GitHub, or PayPal send you to those third-party services. Once you click an outbound link, their privacy policies and terms apply.

    This site sets no first-party cookies. Clipboard actions happen only when you click a copy button.

    Responsible Use

    Use this toolkit only for systems you own, systems you are explicitly authorized to test, bug bounty scopes where the activity is allowed, labs, CTFs, and education.

    The toolkit is provided as-is, without warranty. You are responsible for complying with applicable laws, program rules, contracts, and disclosure requirements.
